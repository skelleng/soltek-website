About SolTek

Founded in 2024, SolTek bridges the gap between cutting-edge solar technology and field execution. We partner with commercial and industrial clients to maximize energy yield, ensure system reliability, and reduce operating costs.

  • Our Mission: Empower businesses to harness solar energy efficiently and sustainably.
  • Our Vision: A world where renewable energy powers every enterprise.
  • Our Approach: Data-driven diagnostics, expert on-site support, and AI analytics.

Our Services

Diagnostics & Repair

Training & Support

Hands-on workshops, live remote guidance, and custom runbooks to upskill your technical teams.

Performance Monitoring

AI Optimization

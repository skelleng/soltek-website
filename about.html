About SolTek

Our Mission

At SolTek, our mission is to empower businesses in the UAE to harness the full potential of solar energy through expert consulting, proactive maintenance, and cutting-edge optimization solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SolTek was born out of a vision to bridge the gap between solar technology advancements and real-world field service execution. Our founders bring decades of experience in renewable energy, network diagnostics, and AI-driven analytics.

Why SolTek?

  • Deep expertise in solar inverter diagnostics and repair
  • Proven track record with commercial and industrial portfolios
  • AI-driven insights to maximize ROI and uptime
  • Dedicated field technician training programs
